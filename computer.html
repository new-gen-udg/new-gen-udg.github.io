Graphics Cards

If we want to game in a computer the main part we need to focus on is the graphic card. Nowadays a good graphics card is more important than ever because we can game in high resolutions as 4k and then the bigest work charge is for this part of our hardware.

In this domaing we have two big choices an Nvidia graphics card or an AMD graphics card. Now we will some benefits of them both.

Nvidia

If you want to buy a graphics card today the most powerfull one you can buy it's an Nvidia branded one, this brand offers the most efficient and powerfull cards that are equipped with the last technologies aswell.

Nvidia is also known for their stable drivers and collaborations with lots of game to make their graphics cards work better on them.

But this pieces of hardware come with a price, so if you planning on buying one you should be prepared for the cost.

AMD

AMD graphics cards aren't as powerfull and technology advanced as the Nvidia ones but they also have lots of fans out there.

AMD is known for having always troubles when launching new products with their drivers and compatibilities, so we recomend that if your planning on adquire and AMD graphics card to better waith some time if is a new realeased product.

We also need to mention that if u let them enought time the drivers start being more stable and then they pull out all the performance of the card.

Initialy the AMD cards are also pricy when realesed but with the past of time their price get lowered and they performance increase, so they become a nice price/performance deal over the Nvidia cards, and that is the time when you should get and AMD card.
