Playstation 5 Normal Edition

Advantages of having the Normal Edition:

  • It has a 4K UHD Blu-ray reader.
  • Having a reader gives us the option of being able to compare prices when buying a game. Being able to choose import, second hand or sales of large stores.
  • Possibility of choosing to buy collector's editions, with figures and physical game.
  • It also has the advantages of digital games, since digital games can be purchased and installed without any problem.
  • The games install much faster, since the base game is not downloaded, but copied from disk and then the updates are downloaded. On slow or faulty connections, physical editing is highly recommended.

Playstation 5 Digital Edition

Advantages of having the Digital Edition

  • It is € 100 cheaper.
  • As it does not have a reader, its design is symmetrical.
  • Absence of reader noise.
  • Greater comfort when changing games, since it does not require changing disc.
  • There is no possibility of the game crashing, as can happen with the physical format.
